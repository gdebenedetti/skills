# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussion technical and specific.
- Respect different levels of experience and backgrounds.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that do not meet this standard.

## Reporting

Report serious issues to the maintainers through GitHub.
